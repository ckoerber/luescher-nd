\title{How to Tune a Contact Interaction}
\input{authors}




\date{\today}

\begin{abstract}
We explain how to tune the coefficient of a contact interaction between nonrelativistic particles on a discretized space in 1, 2, and 3 spatial dimensions.  By taking advantage of a latticized \Luscher zeta function, we can achieve a momentum-independent scattering amplitude at any finite lattice spacing.
\end{abstract}
